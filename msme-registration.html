FAQs

What is the benefit of MSME Registration?

It offers access to government schemes, loans at lower interest, and tax subsidies.

Is Aadhaar mandatory for MSME?

Yes, Aadhaar number of the proprietor/partner/director is required.

Is there any renewal required?

No, Udyam registration is valid for a lifetime unless details change.
