FAQs

Is a Proprietor and Business the same?

Yes, legally they are the same entity and not separate like in companies.

Is PAN card required for registration?

Yes, PAN and Aadhaar are mandatory for registration.

Do I need a GST number?

If your turnover exceeds ₹20 lakh (or ₹10 lakh in special states), GST is mandatory.
