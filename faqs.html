Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and processes.

1. What types of businesses can you help register?

We assist in registering Private Limited Companies, LLPs, OPCs, Sole Proprietorships, and MSMEs.

2. How long does it take to register a company?

Typically 5–7 working days, depending on documentation and MCA processing speed.

3. Can I get GST registration and filing done here?

Yes. We offer end-to-end GST registration, monthly/quarterly filing, and reconciliation support.

4. Do you provide Income Tax Return (ITR) services?

Absolutely. We file ITRs for individuals, freelancers, and companies with accuracy and on-time filing assurance.

5. How do I get support if I need help?

You can reach us anytime via the Contact page, email (support@businessbandhu.com), or WhatsApp (if enabled on the site).

6. Is my data secure on your platform?

Yes, we follow strict privacy protocols and use secure encryption to protect all client data.
