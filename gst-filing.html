FAQs

Who needs to file NIL return?

If your business had no transactions during the month/quarter, you must still file NIL returns.

What happens if I miss a return?

You may face penalties and interest. It's important to file every month even if there's no business activity.

Is reconciliation necessary?

Yes, reconciliation helps avoid mismatches and ensure accurate ITC claims.
