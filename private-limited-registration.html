FAQs

What is the minimum requirement to register a Pvt Ltd company?

Minimum 2 directors and a registered office address in India.

Can foreigners be directors or shareholders?

Yes, at least one director must be an Indian resident. Foreigners can be shareholders.

Is GST mandatory?

GST is optional unless your turnover exceeds ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs in some states).
